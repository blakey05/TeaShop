Our Mission

At Tea Cozy, our mission is to curate a selection of meticulously selected, artisanally crafted, free-range tea leaves sourced sustainably from small batches around the globe. We prioritize fair trade practices, ensuring that the communities producing our tea leaves are treated equitably. Our commitment to organic cultivation means that every sip of Tea Cozy tea is not only delicious but also environmentally friendly, reflecting our dedication to both quality and sustainability in every cup.
